## Category 1: Prediction Market Strategies (Beyond Binary)

### 1.1 Cross-Exchange Arbitrage
- **How:** Same event traded on Polymarket + Kalshi + PredictIt
- **Edge:** Price discrepancies between platforms
- **Risk:** Low (if done right), but requires capital on multiple platforms
- **Example:** Trump election odds at 60% on Polymarket, 55% on Kalshi → buy low, sell high
- **Capital needed:** $500+ to make it worth fees

### 1.2 Market Making
- **How:** Provide liquidity to prediction markets, earn spread
- **Edge:** Capture bid-ask spread on high-volume markets
- **Risk:** Inventory risk (stuck with losing side)
- **Platforms:** Polymarket (2% taker fee starting March 30)
- **Requirement:** Fast execution, inventory management

### 1.3 Calendar Spread Trading
- **How:** Trade same asset across different expiration dates
- **Example:** BTC Up/Down March 29 vs April 5
- **Edge:** Time decay, volatility term structure
- **Risk:** Correlation breakdown

## Category 2: Options Income Strategies

### 2.1 Cash-Secured Puts (Stock Options)
- **How:** Sell put options, collect premium
- **Edge:** Theta decay (time works for you)
- **Risk:** Assigned shares if price drops below strike
- **Requirement:** Alpaca account (already have), options approval
- **Target:** 30-45 DTE, 0.30 delta puts on stable stocks
- **Expected yield:** 1-2% monthly on deployed capital

### 2.2 Covered Calls
- **How:** Own stock, sell call options above current price
- **Edge:** Collect premium + potential upside
- **Risk:** Cap upside if stock moons
- **Best for:** sideways/bearish markets

### 2.3 Credit Spreads
- **How:** Sell spread (bull put or bear call)
- **Edge:** Defined risk, theta decay
- **Risk:** Max loss if wrong direction
- **Example:** Sell $100 put, buy $95 put on AAPL

## Category 4: Active Trading (Non-Binary)

### 4.1 Momentum/Trend Following
- **How:** Buy breakouts, sell breakdowns
- **Edge:** Ride established trends
- **Markets:** Stocks, crypto spot
- **Tools:** Alpaca API (already connected)

### 4.2 Mean Reversion
- **How:** Buy oversold, sell overbought
- **Edge:** Statistical tendency to revert to mean
- **Indicators:** RSI, Bollinger Bands, Z-score
- **Works best:** Range-bound markets

### 4.3 Statistical Arbitrage
- **How:** Trade correlated assets when they diverge
- **Example:** BTC vs ETH correlation breakdown
- **Edge:** Pairs eventually reconverge
- **Risk:** Correlation breakdown (2022 LUNA crash)
