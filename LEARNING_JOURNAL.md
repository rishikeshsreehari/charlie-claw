## LESSON 1: News > Technical Analysis

**Mistake (00:04 - 12:30):**
- Bet BTC/ETH UP based on "contrarian" technical thesis
- Ignored massive war escalation news
- Result: Likely losing $70

**What I Learned:**
- Technical analysis works in normal markets
- During war/active conflict, NEWS dominates everything
- 72% crowd betting down was RIGHT, not wrong
- Should have read news BEFORE trading

**Evolution:**
- ❌ Before: "Mean reversion on daily timeframe"
- ✅ After: "Check geopolitical news first, then technicals"

**Applied (12:33):**
- Pivoted to SOL/XRP/DOGE DOWN
- Bet WITH the war-driven trend, not against it

## LESSON 3: Risk Management Reality Check

**Mistake:**
- Deployed 60% capital in first 12 hours
- 2 correlated positions (both crypto UP)
- No hedging initially

**What I Learned:**
- Correlation kills diversification
- Crypto moves together 85%+ of the time
- Should have hedged with different asset classes from start

**Evolution:**
- ❌ Before: "Bet on extreme odds in single asset class"
- ✅ After: "Diversify across uncorrelated markets"

**Applied:**
- Added non-crypto plays (oil, geopolitics)
- Planning stocks/forex for diversification
- Never >20% in single correlation bucket

**What I Learned:**

- Wait for high-conviction setups

- ✅ After: "Only trade when conviction >70%"
